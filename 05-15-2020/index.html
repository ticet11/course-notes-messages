UML for Developers

What are UML Structural Diagrams?

Structural Diagrams show the structure, of course. They depict which elements are related to which and how everything connects.

What are UML Behavioral Diagrams?

Behavioral Diagrams show how a system is supposed to behave. It shows the dynamic aspects of the program.

What are UML Class Diagram Associations, Multiplicity, and Navigability?

  1. Association: The relationships between classes.
  2. Multiplicity: The direction of the associations. The different requirements for how they can coexist.
  3. Navigability: The way different classes are able to communicate with each other.

What are UML Activity Diagrams?

Graphical depiction of a workflow. It should account for user decisions, iterations in the code, and what other actions are taking place concurrently.

What are UML Use Case Diagrams?

Use Case Diagrams show all of the actions a user can take with your application and how those actions may connect with other users or the system.

What are UML Deployment Diagrams?

Depicts the differenct nodes(hardware components) and artifacts(software components) that will house the application and needed assets for the application and how they relate.

What are UML Package Diagrams?

Shows a packaging of UML elements. A more hierarchical organization of a class diagram.

What are UML Interaction Sequence Diagrams?

Sequence diagrams show how the different objects and classes interact, send and receive messages, to make a certain scenario or use case work.

What are UML State Machine Diagrams?

Show's all relevant activities and objects involved in changing the overall state of your program. For example, if you were making a state machine diagram for your keyboard, you may consider upper and lowercase letters. With the CapsLock key, you can put your keyboard in one of two states. Other buttons could be pressed that would not effect this state.
